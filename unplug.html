Choose a Timeframe

ecide on the duration of your unplugged period. It could be a few hours, a full day, a weekend, or even longer if you prefer. Select a timeframe that fits well with your schedule and commitments.

Set Guidelines

Establish clear guidelines for what "unplugged" means for you. This could include turning off your phone, avoiding social media, not checking emails or work-related messages, and refraining from using any digital devices during this time.

Plan Alternatives

Engage in hobbies, spend time outdoors, read a book, meditate, exercise, or spend quality time with friends and family. Having a plan in place will help you stay focused and resist the temptation to reach for your devices.
